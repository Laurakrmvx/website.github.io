Lana Del Rey

About

Lana Del Rey (born 1985) is an American singer-songwriter known for her cinematic style, nostalgic themes, and unique voice. She gained international fame with “Video Games” (2011) and has since become one of the most influential artists of her generation.

Timeline

  1. 2005 – Began performing under stage names.
  2. 2011 – Released "Video Games".
  3. 2012 – Breakthrough with album *Born to Die*.
  4. 2019 – *Norman Fucking Rockwell!* nominated for Grammy.

Contributions

  • Redefining pop music with cinematic style.
  • Influence on aesthetics in music videos.
  • Blending retro Americana with modern themes.
